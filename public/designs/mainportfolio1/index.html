Admissions Protocol

I. Application

Submit the formal application dossier online or via our admissions office, accompanied by the requisite preliminary documentation.

II. Assessment

Candidates participate in an age-appropriate scholastic evaluation to ensure alignment with our academic standards.

III. Interview

A formal interaction between the prospective student, parents, and the admissions committee to discuss goals and values.

IV. Enrollment

Upon acceptance, final documentation is submitted and the enrollment fee is processed, officially welcoming the student to RMPS.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the student-to-teacher ratio?
We maintain a strict 1:20 student-to-teacher ratio to ensure that every child receives personalized attention and guidance throughout their academic journey.
Do you offer transportation facilities?
Yes, RMPS offers a comprehensive, GPS-tracked fleet of air-conditioned buses covering a 30km radius around the campus for secure student transit.
Which curriculum does the school follow?
The school is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), offering a globally recognized curriculum integrated with advanced pedagogical frameworks.
Are extracurricular activities mandatory?
We strongly encourage participation. Every student is required to choose at least one sport and one cultural club per academic year to foster holistic development.
